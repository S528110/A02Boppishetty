The Ice Cream Company

Since 1979, our shop has been delighting kids and adults of all ages with fantastic homemade ice creams, mouthwatering soups, salads and sandwiches, and a friendly atmosphere that makes everyone feel right at home.

We Have helped countless customers make wonderful memories, and we can not wait to do the same for you! Stop in anytime to enjoy a delicious meal and ice cream in our remodeled dining room, or take home a hand packed quart of your favorite flavor. Our friendly staff, comfortable shop, and the best ice cream in town are guaranteed to please!
